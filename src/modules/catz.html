The Catz' Module

A growing PDF-based module series inspired by The Real ITBJazz Book. This comprehensive learning material provides structured educational content for jazz musicians at various skill levels.

Kurikulum Initiative

About This Module

The Catz' Module represents our commitment to providing structured, sustainable, and effective learning materials. Each module is carefully crafted to align with our curriculum goals and support both individual study and group learning sessions.

This series is part of our larger initiative to achieve 80% member satisfaction within one management year through comprehensive educational resources.
