About Academy ITBJazz

Academy ITBJazz is an initiative to systematically document and share our educational programs, learning modules, and community activities. We believe in structured learning, collaborative expression, and continuous improvement of our members' musical skills.

Through our curriculum-based approach, event-driven learning, and mentorship programs, we aim to create a sustainable and effective learning environment for jazz enthusiasts.
